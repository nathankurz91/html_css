Inside the Start of a Paragraph:

In the middle of a paragraph

This is a sea turtle. Sea turtles have flippers instead of feet.

Image Alignment

This is a sea turtle. Sea turtles have flippers. The rest of this paragraph is simply to show that the text will flow around the images right hand side. The image is placed on the left side of the text.

This is a sea turtle. Sea turtles have flippers. The rest of this paragraph is simply to show that the text will flow around the images left hand side. The image is placed on the right side of the text.

The alignment of pictures can also be done vertically with the "align" tag using the attributes "top", "middle", and "bottom".
